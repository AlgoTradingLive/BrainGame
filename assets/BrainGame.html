CalcMind — Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the CalcMind app ("App") operated by its developer. By using this App, you agree to the terms described below.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

The App is designed to be used without account registration. However, the following data may be collected:

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for proper functioning of the App.
  • Usage Data: Game scores, coins earned, daily progress, and streak data — stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect advertising ID and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Section 4.
  • Voucher Redemption (Optional): If you choose to redeem a Google Play Voucher, you will be directed to an external Google Form where you submit your name, mobile number (WhatsApp), email address, coins earned, and a screenshot of your in-app wallet, solely to verify and process the voucher request. This information is collected and stored via Google Forms/Google Sheets, governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
  • To operate and improve the App's games (Math Booster, Snake Math).
  • To track your coins, daily limits, and streak for a personalised experience.
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To process Google Play Voucher redemption requests (only when submitted by you).
  • To analyse aggregate usage patterns and improve gameplay.
3. DATA STORAGE

All game progress data (User ID, coins, scores, streaks, daily earnings) is stored locally on your device only using browser localStorage. This data is not transmitted to any external server by the App itself.

⚠️ Important — No Backup or Recovery: Your User ID and all associated coins/progress exist only on this device. The App does not back up this data to any server or account. If you uninstall the App, clear app data/cache, or switch to a new device, your User ID and all accumulated coins will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered or restored by the developer.

Maintaining and safeguarding your device (avoiding uninstall, accidental cache/data clearing) to preserve your User ID and coins is solely your responsibility as the user. The developer is not liable for any loss of ID, coins, or progress resulting from app uninstallation, data clearing, device change, device reset, or device loss.

4. ADVERTISING (GOOGLE ADMOB)

This App uses Google AdMob to display banner and video advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including:

  • Advertising ID (GAID / IDFA)
  • App interaction data and crash reports
  • General location (country/region level)
  • Device and network information

Google's data collection is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads via: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

5. COINS & VOUCHER REWARD SYSTEM
  • Coins are earned by playing games, watching video ads, and daily logins.
  • A maximum of 500 coins per day can be earned.
  • Accumulating 15,000 coins makes you eligible to request a Google Play Voucher.
  • Voucher availability is subject to stock and developer discretion.
  • Providing false information during voucher redemption will result in permanent disqualification.
  • One mobile number is permitted to redeem only one voucher; duplicate or repeat requests from the same mobile number will be rejected.

⚠️ Coins have no standalone monetary value until a voucher is officially issued and delivered. Coins are tied to your device's local User ID — see Section 3 regarding permanent loss on uninstall/data clearing.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that personal data of a child under 13 has been inadvertently collected, it will be deleted promptly upon request. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.
